**Song Trend Lifecycle Analysis**
Spotify India Weekly Charts | May 2025 – May 2026

Every music listener in India has experienced both sides — a song that was inescapable for two weeks and then completely gone, and another you barely noticed when it released but were still listening to six months later. This project puts a number to that difference.


**Problem Statement**
The music industry's primary measure of a song's success is a single number: its peak chart rank. This collapses fundamentally different listening behaviors into one metric — a song that debuted at rank 1 and vanished within two weeks looks identical in a headline stat to a song that climbed steadily for three months and held its position for the rest of the year.
This project analyzes 10,798 weekly chart entries across 635 unique songs to classify songs by their actual streaming lifecycle — how fast they rise, how high they peak, and how long they sustain listener attention after that peak. The goal is not to judge artistic merit, but to surface a measurable pattern that labels, independent artists, and marketing strategists can use to make more informed decisions about how success on a streaming chart is defined and tracked.
